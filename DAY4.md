## Biggest Insight

The biggest insight from this exercise was understanding that becoming a Data Analyst or Data Scientist is not only about learning tools but also about building projects, solving real-world problems, and creating a strong portfolio. The roadmap showed me the importance of following a structured learning path instead of randomly learning different technologies.

## Learnings

* Chain-of-Thought prompting generates more detailed and personalized responses.
* Breaking a problem into smaller steps improves AI output quality.
* A structured roadmap helps identify skill gaps clearly.
* Projects and portfolio building are as important as technical skills.

## Observations

* Claude asked relevant questions before generating recommendations.
* The roadmap was more customized than a normal prompt.
* Step-by-step reasoning produced actionable career guidance.
